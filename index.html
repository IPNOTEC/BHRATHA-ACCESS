Dynamic Brotherhood Model

Adding New BROs

The model dynamically adjusts when new BROs join. The formula automatically recalculates ownership percentages based on the new total investment pool.

Example: Adding BRO 4 with ₹60,00,000
New Total Investment = ₹1,60,00,000
BRO 4 gets: (60/160) × 89.2% = 33.450%
Existing BROs' stakes adjust proportionally downward
